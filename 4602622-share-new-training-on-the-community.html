How to share a new training course in the Community?
Written by Claire Philippe
Updated over a week ago

Once your training has been created, and when you publish it, you will be invited to share it in the Community, if desired.

Sharing the training in the Community allows you to notify your learners that a new training is available and thus to promote it.

To share a training in the Community, you must first create your training, then once finalized, click on the Publish button.

A pop-up window inviting you to share the training in the Community will appear.

You can then select the community board(s) on which you want to share the training, depending on the scope concerned.

Example: if the training is available on the internal perimeter as well as a partner perimeter, you will be offered to select one or the other or both.

If you click Skip, you skip this step and your training will not be shared on the Community.

⚠️ If you skip this step and decide not to share the training in the Community, you will not be able to share it afterwards.

To share it in the community, select the desired perimeter(s) and click on Share.

A message including a link to the training concerned will then be published on the selected board.

By clicking on See more, the learner will access the relevant training catalog page.
