Accessing the Rise Up Mobile app

How do I download the Rise Up Mobile app to access my trainings on mobile?
Written by Kevin Legros
Updated over a week ago

Your Rise Up platform becomes easier to access for you and your mobile learners, through the Rise Up Mobile Learning app, available on Google Play and App Store.

Download the app

To download the Rise Up app, simply go on the Google or App store, depending on your device, search for the Rise Up app, and click on Install / Download.

  • Download on Google play

  • Download on the App Store

Sign in to the Rise Up app

Once downloaded, all you need to do is enter your login details to connect to your Rise Up platform and access your Training paths, the Community page, and your Account. You are automatically identified and can access your dedicated space.

You will be asked to enter:

  • the URL link of your Rise Up platform (you will not be asked for this information if you have a white label app - see "Your dedicated app" section of this article)

  • your login

  • your password

As a simpler alternative to logging in with your identifiers, you will be offered to log in by scanning a QR Code. You just have to click on "Scan a QR code".

To find this QR Code on your platform, connect to your platform on your PC, go to "My Account" and then in the "Security" section.

Your dedicated app

To further personalize your mobile space, it is possible to create an app dedicated to your white label platform. This is developed by our Technical team and will later be added to the various stores. You can therefore customize :

  • the logo

  • description

  • visuals

  • and even more !

Do not hesitate to contact your dedicated sales representative to request a dedicated app.
