Export results of learners in Excel format

Last updated on 07/28/2020
Written by Kevin Legros
Updated over a week ago

It is possible to obtain statistics as Excel exports for:

  • One or more users

  • A training

  • A training path

Export of data linked to one or more users

To export data related to a user, you must access his detailed page related to the training concerned.

Export of data linked to one or more training paths

To export data related to a training, all you have to do is export the data of all learners registered for that training.

Access: Side Menu > Training > View, then select the desired training and click on Manage ‚öôÔ∏è to display the Management Page of the selected Training.

Then select all the learners registered for the training and click on Excel Export.

You will then download all the data related to the training concerned.

To export data related to several training courses, you need to access the list of your training courses, by following: Side menu > Training courses > View.

On the training page you can then select the training courses based on the filters available on the right of the screen. You can thus filter the trainings by creator, state, type, category, etc. Once your Filter has been selected, click on Excel Export. You can then download all the data related to the training paths that meet this filter.



Export of data linked to a training path

The procedure is identical than the one used to download data from a training but this time for a learning path.

To export the data linked to a path, all you have to do is export the data of all the learners registered for this path.

Access: Side Menu > Training > View, then select the desired path and click on Manage ‚öôÔ∏è to display the Manage Selected Path Page.

Then select all the learners registered for the course and click on Excel Export.

It is also possible to carry out the same exports of data related to Training registrations, by reproducing the same process via the Training registration Management page.

This page is accessible via the Side menu > Training registrations > View.
