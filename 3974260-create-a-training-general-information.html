Create a training > General information

Last updated on 07/30/2020
Written by Claire Philippe
Updated over a week ago

When creating a training course, the following information must be completed:

  • General information

  • Detailed information (optional)

  • Configuration (optional)

  • Information about the organization (optional)

  • Customized fields (optional)

This article focuses on the General information section.

Title

The title of the training is a required field, which will be included in legal documents, but also in e-mails.

Language

The default language is French. It is possible to choose another language from more than 20 languages available.

Teaching time

Teaching time is the “estimated” time to complete the training. This field is required and must be completed immediately, but it can be edited later if it turns out that your estimate of the teaching time was wrong.


Type

Several choices are offered (depending on the pre-configuration):

  • Multi-scope: this is the choice to be made if you want to create training for users who already have a user account on the Internal Platform and/or on one or more Partner Platforms.

  • Public: this choice is only offered if you have activated the Public Catalog in the Configuration.

What is Public Training?

Public Training is training intended to be offered to an External Learner, meaning that the learner does not have a user account on the Platform. This can be, and often is, paid training.

Principle of access to public training:

Once the public training has been created, an access URL can be retrieved and made available to the “audience” deemed to be interested. The potential learner will then be able to access the Platform to view the Public Training (access to the public training description sheet without needing a User account). If the external Learner decides to take the Public Training offered, he/she then has to register on the Platform and a User account must be created.

  • Anonymous: it is the only type of training that someone can take without having a user account on the Platform (hence the term “anonymous”). Thus, there is no count of the number of licenses for an “anonymous user”.
    ⚠️ The term can be confusing, because “anonymous” training enables the first & last names and e-mail address of the “learner” to be retrieved and even be included in reports on such training. However,

Principle of access to Anonymous Training:

When creating “anonymous” training, a URL link will be generated and can be shared by the administrator with the external user. The user will have to fill in their first & last names and e-mail address, and will then be able to take the training immediately. In general, this feature is used to send questionnaires and quizzes to people outside the company. Typical example: as part of a recruitment process, it is possible to ask everyone who has applied and sent their CVs to complete a quiz, questionnaire or test, to perform an initial screen of the applicants.

“Anonymous” training is “ephemeral” for anyone who takes it. In fact, if someone returns to the access URL sent for their training after a certain period, the training will no longer be accessible and so, for a quiz or questionnaire, it will not be possible to add to the answers.

From the company’s viewpoint, “Anonymous” training does not appear in the Training Catalog (nor in the “Public” Catalog). Administrators will be able to access it through the View Training Page ( Side Panel > Training > View).

The company will be able to view the results of Anonymous training in the same way as other training (“multi-scope” or “public” training courses).

⚠️Once the type of the training created (Multi-scope, Public, Anonymous) has been approved, it can no longer be changed. You will need to duplicate the training to be able to change the Training Type.


Scopes

Only completed for “multi-scope” training. In this case, it is necessary to specify the Scope over which the training being created will be available, i.e. list all Platforms (internal platform and/or one or more partner platforms) on which the training being created will be published.

⚠️ Partner administrators have no choice of types and scopes. They can only publish their training on their partner platform


Designers

By default, the designer’s name displayed is the name of the person who creates the training. It is also possible to change the designer or add one or more other designers. The same training can have several designers. Note that, as with administrators, there is no hierarchy among designers, which means that all designers designated for a given training course have the authority to edit it simultaneously. This requires a minimum degree of coordination between the different designers of the same training.


Image

The ideal image size is 500 x 500 pixels


Banner

The ideal size is 2000 x 500 pixels.

Default images are offered in the platform, but it is also possible to search for images on the Internet, or to import images from your computer. The image and banner are required fields when creating training, to ensure a consistent visual appearance for users when they access the catalog(s) and/or the list of training courses for which they are registered.
