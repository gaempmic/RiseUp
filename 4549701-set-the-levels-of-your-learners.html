Set the levels of your learners

How to create levels for your learners? How to configure the levels created and allow your learners to level up?
Written by Claire Philippe
Updated over a week ago

The Levels aim to award success points to your learners, in order to stimulate your teams and keep track of their progress.

To create and configure the levels available for your learners, go to the Side Menu > Objectives > Levels.


Base level (by default)

Note that a level 0 is created by default. It corresponds to the basic level of your learners who have no success point. It is not possible for you to modify the number of success points linked to this basic objective. They will always remain at zero. And you won't be able to delete it either.

However, you can change its name.

All your new learners will therefore be assigned this basic level.

This is the first to appear in the list of levels.


Create learner levels

Click Create Level on the right side of the screen to create a new level.

For each level created, you can indicate:

  • Level name (required)

  • Description (optional)

  • Success points (required)

Depending on the success points entered for each level, the learner will move on to the next level once he has earned the expected number of success points.

Assign success points to objectives to advance my learners in level

In order to allow learners to earn success points, you will need to assign success points to the objectives you created on the platform.

To learn more, see the article Assign success points to your objectives.

Access to my level as a learner

The learner visualizes his level on the left of his dashboard as well as in the My level section appearing in the horizontal bar at the top of the screen.

The learner also visualizes the number of success points he must obtain in order to move to the next level.
