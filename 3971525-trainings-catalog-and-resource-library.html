Trainings catalog and Resource Library

The catalog allows your learners to discover the training courses available on their platform

The Catalog is accessible via the navigation bar at the top of the page, and gives access to the Training catalog, as well as to the Resource Library.


Access to the Catalog

The catalog groups together all the training courses validated and made visible by the administrator or the designer.

The learner can consult the training courses available to him by sorting and searching according to categories, keywords or skills configured by the administrator.

> Learners can mark trainings as “Favorites”. They will then appear in a dedicated section in the Catalog tab. This will allow them to highlight and report on the training courses they wish to attend or simply identify the training courses that they particularly liked;

> A “New” section allows you to highlight your latest published trainings. This will allow learners to easily identify recently published content;

> Learners will be able to see which training courses have been completed member(s) of their group(s), and thus discover in a more fun way the trainings that could interest them;

To find out more, go to settings of the Training Catalog. These settings are only accessible to Internal Platform Administrators (and not to partner administrators).

Resource Library

The resource library groups together documents (documents of all kinds, texts, videos, iframe, etc.) that can be useful to learners.

These documents can be grouped into folders. The Resource Library works similarly to a kind of an internal Google Drive on the platform.

> Click on the link to find out more about the Resource Library settings. These settings are only accessible to Internal Platform Administrators (and not to partner administrators).

The catalog and the resource library are accessible to users by clicking on the Catalog tab on the left side of the banner on the top of the screen.

In case both the catalog and the resource library have been activated.

After clicking on this button, the user directly accesses the training catalog, with the possibility of switching to the resources library by clicking on the Resources button.
