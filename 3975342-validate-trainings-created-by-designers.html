Validate trainings created by Designers

Last updated on 08/13/2020
Written by Kevin Legros
Updated over a week ago

Two accesses are possible:

  • Direct access from the Dashboard, by clicking in the Pending Actions box

  • Access via : Side menu > Training > Validate trainings

In the previous steps, we saw how to create a training.

Only Administrators (partner administrators) and designers can create a training. However, there is a difference depending on whether the training is created by an Administrator (partner administrator) or by a Designer.

  • When the training is created by an Administrator, as soon as the administrator clicks on the Publish button, the status of the training changes from Draft to Published and can therefore be visible to learners according to the visibility rules that have been defined.

  • On the other hand, in a standard configuration, when the training is created by a Designer, when the latter clicks on the Publish button, the training does not immediately go to the Published state but goes through a Pending validation state.

In the latter case, the Administrator (or partner administrator) must validate the training so that it is published and therefore accessible.

He will access the validation page for pending training (below)

He can then decide whether to:

  • ✅ Validate the training

  • ❌ Refuse to validate the training

The designer will receive an email to notify him (if it has not been deactivated).
