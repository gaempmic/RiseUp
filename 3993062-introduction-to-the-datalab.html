Introduction to the Datalab

Last updated on 07/28/2020
Written by Kevin Legros
Updated over a week ago

A special section is dedicated to Reports and Statistics.

Access : Side Menu > Datalab

There are 2 sub-sections:

  • Online follow-up

  • Data export

Online follow-up

Online follow-up gives access to reports presented visually with different types of graphs.

In this section you can find :

  • “General Report”, available to Administrators, Partner Administrators, Creators and Group Managers, based on their respective scope.

  • “Dashboards by topic”, currently only available to Internal Administrators.

Data Export

Data export gives you access to:

  • Excel exports by theme, to be chosen from a list, with a sorting / filtering function allowing the Excel Export to be limited to the scope chosen by the administrator. ℹ️ This section is not available to partner Administrators.

  • Personalized exports, which you can configure according to your needs and automatically receive by email on a weekly or monthly basis.

Access to these exports is open to Administrators, Partner Administrators, Creators and Group Managers, according to their respective scope.
