Manage registration requests (made by form)

Last updated on 08/04/2020
Written by Claire Philippe
Updated over a week ago

In the Settings section, you have the option of enabling learners and / or administrators to make requests for training that would not be available in your catalog.

For more information, see the article Setting - Training Catalog, section Activate training requests in the catalog & Activate training requests on the training administration page.

To access training requests by form : Side menu > Training registrations > Manage training requests

Two settings are possible:

  • Possibility for a learner to express an individual training request (from the Catalog)

  • Possibility of expressing training requests for a group of learners

Opportunity for a learner to express an individual training request (from the Catalog)

A learner cannot find the training actually searched for in the training catalog.

During Pre-Configuration of the Platform ⚙️, learners can be given the option of expressing their training needs by posting a request to administrators. To do so, simply check the check box Activate training requests in the catalog.

To request registration for training not included in the Catalog, learners can then click You can’t find your training (outlined in red on the Training Catalog screenshot below).

A form will be opened where learners can express their training needs.

There are 2 conventional cases:

  • Either the learner makes a simple request to feedback new training needs to the Platform administrators, in the hope that this request will lead to creating suitable new training or searching for existing training offered by a training organization outside the company.

  • Or the learner has identified existing training already offered by an external organization and completing this form means that the learner is requesting registration for this external training, the references to which can be specified in the form. This is then a separate training registration request mode.

Opportunity to express training requests for a group of learners

It is also possible for administrators (internal and partner), designers or group managers to express training requests, if the pre-configuration of the platform allows it, i.e. if the check box below was checked during Pre-Configuration : Activate training requests on the training administration page)

Administrators (internal or partner), designers or group managers may actually notice that they don’t find suitable training to train certain learners.

Subject to the Platform’s pre-configuration, they can express their training needs from the training administration page by clicking “Can’t find your training?” outlined in red on the screen below.

A training request form then opens. It is similar to the form presented above for a request made by a learner, except that administrators (designers or group managers) must specify in the request form which users will be registered for the training if it is actually implemented.


Approval or Refusal of training requests

Once the training request form (whether it is an individual request from a learner or a request for a group coming from an administrator, designer, or group manager) has been completed and approved by the applicant, Administrators and Group Managers will see an alert in their dropdown Management Menu, in the Training Registrations Section under the Manage Training Requests sub-section.

By clicking Manage training requests, they can access the screen for managing training requests, where they can directly either approve or refuse the training request.

On this interface, they can view and search for old training requests that were previously approved or refused.
