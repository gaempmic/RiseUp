What notifications learners receive via mobile?
Written by Claire Philippe
Updated over a week ago

Like all iOs and Android apps, the Rise Up Mobile Learning app notifies your learners through push notifications on their smartphones.

Notification windows may therefore appear informing them of actions carried out on the platform that concern them.

The following actions are notified to learners by push notifications on mobile:

  • Validation of a training registration

  • Registration for training by an administrator

  • Reminder of an upcoming face-to-face session (sent 2 days before the start of the session)

  • Live or delayed evaluation available

  • Exam corrected

  • Synchronization of Scorm-type steps (offline)

  • Sending answers for a quiz, exams or surveys (offline)

As a reminder, the mobile app is exclusively made for learner use (no administration feature). Therefore, so push notifications will always be intended for learners.
