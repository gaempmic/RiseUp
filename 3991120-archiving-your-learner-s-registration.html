Archiving your learner's registration

Last updated on 07/27/2020
Written by Claire Philippe
Updated over a week ago

You can archive your learner's registration to a training.

The objective is to allow the learner to completely restart his training, while allowing you to keep the statistics (results and other follow-up data) linked to his previous registration.
