Help center

By clicking the Help Center, administrators have access to the Rise Up Help Center presenting all information and features on the platform.

Online help chat

If you are an administrator, the icon above is available at the bottom right of the screen on every page. This is online help chat, allowing you to put questions to Rise Up teams.

Access to Rise Up Café

At any time, if you are an administrator, partner administrator, designer or community manager, you can access our discussion space dedicated to Rise Up and more generally the training world. It has tutorials on the Rise-Up Platform and on new features implemented, as well as countless tips on best training practices.

Rise Up Café is accessible:

  • Either from the thumbnail above, located on the right of your screen, by scrolling down from your Dashboard.

  • Or by clicking Rise Up Café in the window that opens by clicking your first name or profile picture at top right of the screen-top banner.
