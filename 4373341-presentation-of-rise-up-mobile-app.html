Presentation of Rise Up Mobile app

What features are available on the mobile app? How to access the catalog on the app?
Written by Kevin Legros
Updated over a week ago

To download and log into the Rise Up Mobile app on your phone, see the dedicated article "Accessing the Rise Up Mobile App".

The Rise Up Mobile app consists of 5 different sections accessible from the horizontal menu located at the bottom of your app.

  • Home (Dashboard)

  • Community

  • Learning sprint

  • Catalog

  • My account

The application allows learners to access their account and therefore their training directly on their mobile.

⚠️ Administrator functions (user management, training creation, etc.) are not available in the mobile application.

Home (dashboard)

On your dashboard, you will find:

  • News from your Company. To learn more about how to configure these news, see the article "Customizing the platform", section "News".

  • The trainings you have registered for.

  • If you are registered for classroom sessions, your upcoming sessions will also appear on your dashboard.

Community

Your Community space allows you to access your exchange spaces, see the latest messages posted and also write messages.

By clicking on the side menu at the top left, you will access the various discussion boards.

Learning sprint

The Sprint section allows the learner to initiate "Learning Sprints", curated revision programs.

First, the learner has to enter the time he wishes to dedicate to his learning Sprint (5, 10 or 15 minutes). And then the app will create for him, based on the duration selected, a curated revision program. The content will be selected randomly among the trainings that he has already completed.

Once he has selected what he wants to revise, he just has to click on "Let's go!" to launch his learning sprint.

Catalog

The Catalog section provides access to all the trainings available to the learner within the training catalog of his company. Depending on the settings selected, the learner can view the description of the training courses, and register directly.

ℹ️ The learner does not have access to the Resource Library on the Mobile app.

My account

In the My account section, learners can access their learning statistics and modify their settings (modify their photo, language, etc.).
