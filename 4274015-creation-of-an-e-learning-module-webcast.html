There's no need to record yourself via another tool and then integrate it into Rise Up,. Webcast allows you to film yourself and record yourself vocally directly on the platform.

The webcast will allow you, for example, to make a video presenting the training, in which case it will be placed in the first step.

Click on Start recording.

A window allowing you to start recording will appear. On first use, a window may open on your browser asking you to allow the tool to access your webcam and your microphone.
