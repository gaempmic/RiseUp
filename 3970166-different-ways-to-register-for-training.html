Different ways to register for training

Last updated on 07/24/2020
Written by Claire Philippe
Updated over a week ago

To take a training, learners must be registered to at least one of them (as soon as they are registered for a training, it appears on their Dashboard and learners only have to click Begin to start their training).

There are 3 different registration methods :

  • Mode 1/ Direct registration -> done by implementing smart rules (typically -> for the Newcomers group) or by one-off registration by the administrator/partner administrator or by creators/designers for trainings they have created themselves

  • Mode 2/ Registration request by the employee from the Catalog -> Awaiting approval by an Administrator or a Group Manager -> once the registration request has been approved, the relevant training will immediately appear in the learner’s Dashboard, who will only have to click BEGIN.

  • Mode 3/ Training request via a Form -> for external training given by external training organizations (generally classroom sessions) that you wish to highlight.

Direct registration (Mode 1)

Learners have nothing to do. They will have direct access to their training, as listed on their Dashboard.

Registering for training from the Catalog (Mode 2)

Learners can search the Catalog for training for which they want to register. A search tool on the left of the screen makes it easier to search for training (sorted by category, session dates, location, etc.).

By clicking See more for one of the training courses presented in the Catalog, learners have access (below) to the training description sheet containing a description, objectives of the training, and the various modules. They also see the different opinions that may have been posted about the training by other learners.

By clicking Sign Up, learners initiate a training request that will be forwarded to the administrators (or partner administrators, as applicable, if the learner is a partner learner) and possibly to their Group Manager.

Registration for training not included in the Catalog (Mode 3)

To request registration for training not included in the Catalog, learners can click on Can’t find your training ? (outlined in red in the screenshot below).

⚙️ For this prompt to appear, the Administrator must have previously configured the platform accordingly (checkbox “Activate training requests in the catalog” must have been checked in the Configuration).

A form will appear, in which learners can express their training needs and describe any external training that might interest them.

Once the form has been completed and submited, the request is forwarded to the administrators (or partner administrators, as applicable, if the learner is a partner learner) and possibly to their Group Manager.
