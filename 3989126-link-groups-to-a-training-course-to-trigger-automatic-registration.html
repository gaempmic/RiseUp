Link Groups to a training course to trigger automatic registration

How to set up automatic registrations for training courses?
Written by Kevin Legros
Updated over a week ago

Only Administrators, Partner Administrators, and Designers can link groups to a course.

Access: Lateral Menu > Training > View in the thumbnail of the training selected, scroll down to the bottom part of the page dedicated to Users, then click the Linked Groups button to switch from managing registered users to managing linked groups.

Screenshot - bottom part of the Manage training page.

The window below is displayed to select the groups you want to link to the training.

Simply click the Valdiate button to link the selected groups to the training and see the new groups linked to the training appear in the box outlined in green.

Principle of Groups Linked to Training:

When a user group is linked to a training course, it means that any user who is a member of that group or becomes a member in the future will be registered for the training automatically.

Note that if Users are subsequently removed from the group, they will not be unregistered from the training for which they were automatically registered.
