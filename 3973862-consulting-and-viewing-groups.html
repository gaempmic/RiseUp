Consulting and viewing groups

How do I access the list of members of my group? How to access the list of training courses / courses to which they have registered?
Written by Kevin Legros
Updated over a week ago

To view a group you will have to go to the Side Menu > Groups > View

If the user is a Group Manager, he will only be able to view the group(s) for which he is a manager.

As Administrator, you see all the platform groups (on your perimeter).

From this screen, he will be able to :

  • Contact all members of his Group : the user will be able to write his email directly in a pop-up that appear

  • Display related training : this is the list of trainings to which group members are automatically registered when they are integrated into the group

  • Display related paths : this is the list of training paths to which group members are automatically registered when they are integrated into the group

  • Display the list of group members

As an Administrator or Partner Administrator, you will also be able to:

  • Modify a Group ✏️

  • Delete a Group
