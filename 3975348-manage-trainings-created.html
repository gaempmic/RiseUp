Manage trainings created

Last updated on 08/13/2020
Written by Kevin Legros
Updated over a week ago

Access: Side Menu > Trainings > View

You will arrive on the Training consultation page, which is accessible to Administrators, Partner administrators, Designers, Group managers.

This training consultation page presents the list of training courses that you can consult, each training being presented in the form of a horizontal band (thumbnail) showing the main training information (see above).

All training courses are presented, regardless of their state (draft, awaiting validation, published) and their type (multi-perimeter, public, anonymous).

Sort function

A Sort function on the right side of the screen allows you to select the desired training according to the following criteria:

  • Presence of copies to be corrected in training

  • Presence of physical sessions in training

  • Archived training

  • State of training: Published, Draft, Pending validation,

  • Accessibility of training: Visible, Invisible, Inaccessible

ℹ️ Training in 'Draft' or 'Awaiting validation' state is necessarily inaccessible. Once 'published', a training course can be either Visible (in the Catalog) or Invisible, depending on what has been configured in the description of the training course when it was created.

  • Type of training: Internal, Partner (associated with a specific Partner), Public, Anonymous, Imported training

  • Training categories and sub-categories

  • Training creators/designers

  • Training path to which the training is linked

  • Keywords associated with the training

  • If this category has been selected upstream in the configuration : Custom fields associated with training

Presentation of a training thumbnail

Directly on the training thumbnail, appear :

  • Pending corrections: With a single click, it is possible to go directly to the exam correction management page

  • Physical modules: With a single click, you can go directly to the classroom session management page

In the top right corner of each thumbnail, you can access the different management options :

  • ✏️ Modification of the training

  • ⚙️ Training management (see focus below): Allows access to the Training Management Page where it will be possible:

> To consult the training statistics

> To manage the translations of the training (if internalization has selected in the configuration upstream -> the 'Activate training translations' box has been checked)

> To manage classroom sessions (if applicable)

> To register users for Training

> To link user groups to this training (allows to register automatically any user included in a user group, to the training - NB: there will be no unsubscription once the user has registered if the latter is subsequently deleted from the group linked to the training)

  • 📌 Pin a training to move it to the top of the list: To find it easily, it is always possible to display at the top of the screen the training(s) on which you are most likely to work. All you have to do is pin the training by clicking on the icon, which will become darker when the training is pinned. Unpin the training by clicking again on the same icon.

  • [3 vertical dots] Shows more management options:

Available training management options

  • Preview: By clicking on Preview you will be taken to a page where you can view the training as learners can see it and you can complete the training yourself by clicking on Start to appreciate the user experience for learners.

  • Duplicate: By clicking on "Duplicate", you access the training creation / modification page, with a green ephemeral message indicating that you have successfully duplicated the training. If you leave the page just after having duplicated the training, no problem, a duplicate of your training will have been created. You will find it in a 'draft' state in the list of your training courses with the mention (copy) (by returning to Side Menu > View > Training). It will look like the first training in every way, but will have a new creation date.

  • Manage visibility: By clicking on Manage visibility ’, you will bring up the following window where you can choose whether you want the training to be Accessible or Inaccessible.

> Inaccessible: The training will not appear in any Catalog. It will only be accessible in the Training Consultation Page.

> Accessible: In this case, it is possible to manage the visibility of the training, either positively (by defining the users and / or groups of users for whom the training will be visible in the Catalog) or exclusively ( by defining the only users and / or groups of users for whom the training will not be visible in the Catalog).

  • Archive: By clicking on Archive, after a double validation (a pop-up appears to validate that you have not made a wrong manipulation), you archive the training definitively. You will be able to find the training (in particular by sorting to select only the archived training) in your Training consultation page but it will become permanently inaccessible and you will no longer be able to register anyone for this training.

  • Delete: By clicking on Delete, after a double validation (a pop-up appears to confirm that you have not done a wrong manipulation), you permanently delete the training. It won't be visible anywhere, not even on your Training list Page.
