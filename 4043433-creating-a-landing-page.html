Creating a landing page

How to share a training to people outside your organization and invite them to register for a training?
Written by Claire Philippe
Updated over a week ago

Rise-up gives you the possibility of creating a landing page for your training: you will be able to create a landing page, presenting the training, allowing you to distribute it, promote it and invite the targeted audience to register for the training.

You will be able to:

  • Share a training program with users who do not have an account on your Rise Up platform.

  • Invite them to register for the training concerned and thereby create an account on your platform.

This page is therefore intended to be distributed to prospects, customers, or partners who do not have an account on your Rise Up platform.

Creating a landing page / dedicated page

First, create the training you want to make accessible through a landing page (complete its description and training content).

To learn more about creating a course, see the Create a training article.

In the Training configuration section, check the Dedicated (/Landing) page box.

If you want learners to be able to register for training from this dedicated page, also check the Allow registrations box in this same Configuration section.

Be sure to check the "Visible", "Dedicated page" and "Allow registrations" options to ensure that your training runs smoothly.

To share this landing page, retrieve the corresponding URL by accessing the list of your Trainings, then click on More options (3 vertical dots) on the training concerned, and finally click on the URL of the dedicated page to access the URL and copy / paste it (this link is accessible for administrators & designers).

A pop-up window displaying the URL of the page appears.

Copy and paste this URL to share it to your targeted audience.

Users will be able to access the training presentation page in order to discover its content and, depending on your parameters, will be able to register to the training.

A dedicated page displays the following information:

  • Training title

  • Description of the training (you can add images, videos, etc.) into the description text.

  • Objectives

  • Training program (details of modules included in the training)

  • Information related to training (designer, duration, etc.)

Note that the button inviting the user to register / access the training will be different depending on the settings of your platform and the configuration of the training.
