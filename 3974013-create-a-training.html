Create a training

Last updated on 07/30/2020
Written by Claire Philippe
Updated over a week ago

To access the training creation section, follow Side Menu > Training > Create.

Only admins, partner admins, and designers can create a course.

To create training, you need to:

  • Stage 1 - Complete the training description and configuration information -> Training Description Sheet

  • Stage 2 - Create the Training content

You will need to complete the sections below which you will find detailed in the dedicated articles:

  • General information

  • Detailed information (optional)

  • Configuration (optional)

  • Information about the organization (optional)

  • Customized fields (optional)

Once all these sections are filled in, click the button Next to save the data entered and move on to Stage 2.

Once you have clicked the Next button, your Training will be saved in Draft status and you will be able to find it again to edit it or continue working on it from the View Training page (Side Menu > Training > View)
