How to generate my legal documents: attendance sheet, training agreement, etc. ? Which documents are generated automatically?
Written by Kevin Legros
Updated over a week ago

Some documents are generated automatically and others must be generated manually.

It is possible to modify and personalize all of these documents.


Documents generated automatically

The documents generated automatically are as follows:

  • Invitation to a classroom session

  • Attendance certificate

  • Certificate of end of training

  • Statement of objective

  • Certificate of end of training path

These documents are all generated automatically without any action on your part.

You will find the history of the documents generated via Side Menu > Legal Documents > View.

From this page, you will be able to download them by clicking on the arrow üì• corresponding to the desired document.



Generate a training contract

The training contracts for classroom sessions must be generated manually.

Access: Side Menu > Trainings > View > Manage ‚öôÔ∏è > Manage Classroom sessions > Generate training contract

A modal to generate a training agreement allows you to select the classroom session for which you want the contract to be generated.

You'll need to :

  • select the session(s) concerned

  • choose the learners concerned

  • then complete all the required fields.

By clicking on Submit, the contract will be generated and its download will start immediately.


Generate an attendance sheet

You'll have to go to the Management of classroom sessions page.

Access : Side menu > Training > Manage classroom sessions

By clicking on More options (three vertical dots) for the desired session, you can then click on Attendance sheet.

From this page, you can :

  • Sign the attendance sheet

  • Download the attendance sheet in PDF


For more details, see Manage the attendance sheet.

Generate a training program

From the Catalog, by accessing a training you can Download the training program in PDF format by clicking on the dedicated button on the right side of the training page.

This program includes all the information related to the training (if these have been completed): description, duration, objectives, etc.
